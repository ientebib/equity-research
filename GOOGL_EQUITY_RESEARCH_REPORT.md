### Business Description

Alphabet Inc. is a holding company that owns and operates Google, Waymo, Verily, and other technology ventures. Google is the world's largest search engine and a dominant player in digital advertising. The company has expanded into cloud computing, autonomous vehicles, life sciences, and fiber broadband through its "Other Bets" division.
